Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Roamly ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

Roamly stores the locations you mark as visited, along with any notes, photos, and memories you add. This data is stored locally on your device and synced via iCloud to your other devices.

If you sign up for our mailing list or contact us, we collect your email address and any information you provide in your message.

We may collect basic, anonymous usage analytics to help us improve the app experience.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Display your 3D globe, visited locations, and travel journal entries
  • Sync your data across your devices via iCloud
  • Send you updates if you subscribe to our mailing list
  • Respond to your messages if you contact us

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

iCloud & Data Storage

Roamly uses Apple's iCloud to sync your journal data across devices. Your data is stored securely using Apple's infrastructure and encryption. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

Photos & Location Data

Photos you add to journal entries are stored locally and in iCloud. Location data (the places you mark as visited) is used solely within the app to display your travel map. We do not track your real-time location.

Sharing Features

When you share a snapshot of your globe to Instagram or other platforms, the image is generated locally on your device. We do not receive or store shared content.

Your Choices

You can disable iCloud sync at any time in iOS Settings. You can unsubscribe from emails at any time using the link in any email. If you delete the app, locally stored data is removed from your device.

Children's Privacy

Roamly is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us at alex14manning@gmail.com.
