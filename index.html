Plan trips

A day-by-day builder for the way you actually travel.

Time-blocked schedules with six activity types — places, food, activities, flights, housing, and notes. Add photos, links, and details to every stop. Roamly keeps everything in sync across your devices.

  • Six block types for every kind of plan
  • Photos, notes, links & precise times
  • Auto-saves & syncs in real time
Track

An interactive 3D globe for every adventure.

Pin the cities you've visited and explore them on a beautiful, satellite-rendered globe. Spin, zoom, and tap any pin to revisit your trip — your travel history, made tangible.

  • Real-world satellite globe powered by Apple Maps
  • Country & city tracking with one tap
  • See visited countries highlighted on the globe
Discover

Real itineraries from real travelers.

Browse trips published by the Roamly community. Search by destination, sort by top-rated or newest, and bookmark anything that inspires your next adventure.

  • Community-published trips, ranked by votes
  • Filter by destination, duration & activities
  • Save trips to revisit when you're ready to plan
Share

Publish your trips. Inspire someone else.

Share your itineraries publicly with one tap, or send a private link to a friend. See how many travelers loved your trip and watch your library of journals grow.

  • One-tap publish to the Roamly community
  • Private share links for friends & family
  • Track hearts & activity on every post
